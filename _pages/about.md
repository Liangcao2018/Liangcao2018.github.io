## About Me

I am a **Postdoctoral Fellow** in [Chemical and Biological Engineering](https://chbe.ubc.ca/) at the **University of British Columbia**, co-supervised by [Prof. Bhushan Gopaluni](https://dais.chbe.ubc.ca) (Fellow of the Canadian Academy of Engineering) and [Prof. Fengqi You](https://www.engineering.cornell.edu/faculty-directory/fengqi-you) (AAAS/AIChE/FRSC Fellow) at **Cornell University**.

I received my Ph.D. (2024) in Chemical and Biological Engineering from UBC, advised by [Prof. Bhushan Gopaluni](https://dais.chbe.ubc.ca) and [Prof. Yankai Cao](https://optimal.chbe.ubc.ca/). Before that, I was a Postdoc (2024–2025) at the **Massachusetts Institute of Technology** with [Prof. Richard D. Braatz](https://cheme.mit.edu/profile/richard-d-braatz/) (Member of the US National Academy of Engineering, IEEE/IFAC/AAAS/AIChE Fellow). I obtained my B.S. and M.S. from [Beijing University of Chemical Technology](https://www.buct.edu.cn/) advised by [Prof. Youqing Wang](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Youqing-Wang-2), and was a joint M.S. student at the [University of Duisburg-Essen](https://www.uni-due.de/en/) with [Prof. Steven X. Ding](https://www.uni-due.de/aks/stevending_en.php).

My research focuses on **trustworthy machine learning** and its industrial applications, spanning causal discovery, process monitoring & fault diagnosis, soft sensor modeling, and fault-tolerant control. My work has been applied to refinery digital twins, biopharmaceutical manufacturing, lithium-ion battery management, and smart energy systems.
